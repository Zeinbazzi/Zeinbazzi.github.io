About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the Argelander Institute for Astronomy at the University of Bonn, with a primary research focus on the properties and evolution of molecular clouds across different types of galaxies. My work aims to better understand how these clouds form, change, and influence star formation in diverse galactic environments. In addition to this, I have a strong interest in active galactic nuclei and how they affect their host galaxies.
